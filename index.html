60-Minute Summer Workout Program

Designed for Ectomorphs & Endomorphs | 4–6 Days/Week

Program Purpose

This summer fitness program is tailored to help individuals with ectomorph and endomorph body types improve strength, endurance, and overall fitness. The program focuses on muscle-building, fat-loss, and cardiovascular conditioning using a combination of HIIT, circuit training, and compound movements.

Targeted Fitness Components

  • Muscular strength
  • Muscular endurance
  • Cardiovascular fitness
  • Flexibility and core stability

Workout Format

Workouts follow a rotating HIIT + bodyweight training format. Each day features:

  • 1 Cardio Exercise
  • 1 Lower Body Exercise
  • 1 Upper Body Exercise
  • 1 Core Exercise
  • + Additional functional exercises

Non-Workout Activity

Activity: 3v3 basketball with friends (once a week)
Location: Local community gym
Why: Improves agility, cardio, and promotes social motivation and consistent movement.

Nutrition Adjustments

Ectomorph

  • Increase complex carbs (e.g. oatmeal, rice) to support muscle growth.
  • Add 2–3 protein-rich snacks per day (e.g. Greek yogurt, eggs, protein shakes).

Endomorph

  • Cut processed sugars and replace with fruit and vegetables.
  • Implement portion control and high-fiber meals to manage hunger and metabolism.
